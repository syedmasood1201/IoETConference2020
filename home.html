About the Event

A promising IoET Conference that aims to gather research, projects, innovations and ideas incorporating Robotics, Environmental Sensing, Home automation etc. under one roof along with frequently accessible services of AI,data analytics, embedded systems and ML which have revolutionized the tech industry, reducing the complexities and increasing the technicalities. Being an Open Community Flagship event, it connects people across the globe and uplifts unique innovations, rendering an international forum to researchers, academicians, professionals and students for their best works in AI, ML and IoT.
